# Hey, I'm Emy 👋

*Former enfant terrible. Now, a mostly harmless contemporary iconoclast — and part-time aspiring mystic.*



In all seriousness, I'm a researcher, program builder, and writer working at the intersection of **AI, ethics, inclusion, and communication**. I'm Colombian, based in Aachen (Germany), but thinking and working globally.

My work lives in the space where technology meets people, that is, how organizations adopt AI responsibly, how digital tools actually get used (or don't), and what it means ethically to build and deploy intelligent systems in a world that is still figuring all of this out.

I'm currently completing a **Master's in Philosophy and Theology with a focus on AI ethics, alignment and decision** at the University of Lucerne, while working on AI program design and deployment with organizations across Europe, Latin America, and the Caribbean.


## What you'll find here

- 🧠 **AI ethics & governance frameworks** — working documents, reflections, and structured thinking from my research and practice
- ⚙️ **AI workflows & tools** — prompts, automations, and things I've built using Claude, ChatGPT, Zapier, Make, and Lovable
- ✍️ **Writing & research** — templates, analytical frameworks, and knowledge products from my program and consultancy work
- 🌱 **Works in progress** — I believe in learning in public, so some of this will be messy and unfinished. That's the point.



## A bit more about me

By day I design and deploy AI adoption programs for civil society organizations and media institutions, helping non-technical teams understand, govern, and actually use AI tools in their daily work. Before that: journalism, media development, strategic communications, and a fellowship in the US researching digital media and post-conflict narratives in Colombia.

By night (and on Substack): stories, essays, and chronicles about life in all its facets, fiction stories, current affairs, pop culture, internet culture, and of course AI! 

I write in Spanish, think in English, and occasionally dream in Portuguese.
